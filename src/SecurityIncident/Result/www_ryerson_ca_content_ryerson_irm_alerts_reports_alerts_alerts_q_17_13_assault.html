2013-08-22

Assault

Incident Details:

On Wednesday 21 August, 2013 Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 3:40 pm a female and male subject approached a female victim in the southeast area of the Quad. The female subject was observed striking the victim several times in the head with her fists, while the male subject was videotaping the assault. The reporter yelled at the subjects to stop, which they did. The subjects were last seen heading north through the 43 Gerrard Street archway, while the victim headed south through the 50 Gould Street archway.

 

Security and Emergency Services searched the area for the victim and subjects, but unfortunately could not locate them.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
