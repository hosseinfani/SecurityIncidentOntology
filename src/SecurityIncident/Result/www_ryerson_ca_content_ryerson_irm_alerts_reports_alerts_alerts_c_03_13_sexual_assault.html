2013-03-26

Sexual Assault

Incident Details:

On Friday 22 March, 2013 Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

On Thursday 21 March, 2013 at approximately 09:40 am a female student was at 10 Dundas Street West to attend class in the AMC Theatres.  As she approached the 1st floor lobby escalators a male stepped in front of her and prevented her from passing him.  He then grabbed her face with both hands and attempted to kiss her.  The victim was able to escape before he succeeded.  The subject then left and exited onto Dundas Street. 

 

No one was physically injured.  A report was filed with 10 Dundas Security and Toronto Police Services.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by sexual violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

 

Suspect information:

Male
