2013-04-09

Assault

Incident Details:

On Monday 08 April, 2013 Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 9:35 pm a female student was walking on the north side of Gould Street between 40 Gould and Victoria Laneway.  A man was walking towards her, and then ran at her and knocked her to the ground.  The man then walked away towards Yonge Street and left the area.  A passerby assisted the student and Security was notified.  The area was searched but the man was not located.

 

The victim had minor physical injuries but did not require medical attention.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

 

Suspect information:

Male

Dark Complexion

6’2” / 1m 88 cm tall

Thin build

Approximately 45 years of age

Short black hair

Wearing a cream-coloured hooded jacket with a fur-lined hood, dark blue jeans, running shoes

Was initially walking slowly with odd movements, would turn sideways and make indistinct noises, and then continue. After the assault he appeared to walk normally.
