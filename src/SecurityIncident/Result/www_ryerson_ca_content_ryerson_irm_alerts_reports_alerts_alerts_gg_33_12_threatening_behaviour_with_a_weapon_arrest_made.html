2012-09-18

Threatening Behaviour with a Weapon Arrest Made

Incident Details:

On Monday 17 September, 2012 at approximately 1:20 pm Security and Emergency Services received a call from staff at the Ram in the Rye Pub.  Staff reported that a male subject had been displaying a knife in the pub and had been asked to leave as a result of this.  The subject then threatened one of the staff members with the knife, and left the area. The staff member was not physically injured.

 

Security immediately notified the police and located the subject on Bond Street near 125 Bond Street.  Security spoke with the male and was able to retrieve the knife from him.  Shortly thereafter Toronto Police Services arrived took a suspect into custody.  The subject has been identified as a Ryerson student. 

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.   

 

Support for students impacted by assault is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
