2013-12-09

Possible Sexual Assault

Incident Details:

On Friday 06 December, 2013 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident by several community members:

 

At approximately 05:04 PM community members reported loud yelling coming from the 4th floor P1 stairwell, in the Podium building.

 

Information from various witnesses suggests that the subject was seen in the P1 stairwell pulling his pants up, then chasing a woman whose shirt appeared ripped, before fleeing towards Yonge Street.  The woman told some witnesses while passing “he is trying to rape me” and other witnesses reported being approached by a woman on the 1st floor and being advised that she had been assaulted by the subject who had initially offered her alcohol. The woman then stated she had to leave and left northbound on Victoria laneway.

 

Security & Emergency Services patrolled the area, but could not locate either the subject or the woman. Toronto Police Services were notified of the incident.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security. 

 

Support for students impacted by sexual violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
