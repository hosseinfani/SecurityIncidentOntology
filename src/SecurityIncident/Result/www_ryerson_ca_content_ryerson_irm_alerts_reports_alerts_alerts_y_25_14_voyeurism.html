2014-08-14

Voyeurism

Incident Details:

On Monday 11 August, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

At approximately 12:30 PM while a male student was about to use a washroom stall on the 8th floor of the Library and Archives building he saw someone holding a black phone, with the camera facing his stall, on the gap between the stall separator and the stall wall. The student left the stall and used the urinals. The student then exited the washroom and the 2nd floor and immediately notified Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services.

Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services attended the area and on checking the washroom found it vacant. No subject description was obtained.

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

Support for students impacted by sexual violence is available through the Centre for Student Development and Counselling (416-979-5195),(http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
