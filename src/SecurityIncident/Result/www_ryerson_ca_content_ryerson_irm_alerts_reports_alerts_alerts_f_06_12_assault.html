2012-02-13

Assault

Incident Details:

On Saturday 11 February, 2012 at approximately 1:40 am three students reported the following incident to Security and Emergency Services;


The students took a taxi from an off-campus location to the ILLC residence

at 133 Mutual Street, arriving there at approximately 01:30 am.   While

paying the fare, the taxi driver made inappropriate sexual comments to the

students.   One of the students confronted him on the inappropriateness of

this and tried to exit the taxi; the driver then tried to hit her in the face with a closed hand.  The student blocked the punch with her hands.  The students were then able to leave, entered the ILLC and called Security.

The taxi driver drove away prior to Security's arrival.


The victim was not injured. Toronto Police Services attended and are investigating the incident.  The company name and taxi number were provided to the police. The students were only able to obtain a vague description of the subject.


If you have any information about this incident or are the victim of a similar occurrence, please contact Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040.


Suspect information:

Male

Medium complexion

Thin build

Receding dark hair


Notification Information:

If you are a witness to a crime, please call Ryerson Security at 416-979-5040, or Toronto Police Services through Crime Stoppers at 416-222-TIPS (8477).
