Security Incident on Campus - 03 September 2014

 

On Wednesday 20 August, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 04:30 PM a community member was studying in the Library and Archives building on the 7th floor at a cubicle. The victim while studying kept tapping something with her feet and upon checking underneath the desk noticed the subject sitting on the floor, in the adjacent cubicle. The victim immediately began packing up her belongings, and while doing this felt someone touching her feet. The victim left the area and notified Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services of the incident.

 

The victim did not want Toronto Police Services involved. Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services searched the area for the subject, but he was not located.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
