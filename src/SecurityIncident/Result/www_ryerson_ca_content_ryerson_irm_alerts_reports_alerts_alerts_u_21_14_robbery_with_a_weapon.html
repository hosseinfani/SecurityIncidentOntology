2014-07-23

Robbery with a Weapon

Incident Details:

On Wednesday 23 July, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

At approximately 01:00 AM a male Ryerson student was smoking in the Kerr Hall Quad by the Northeast alcove. The subjects approached the victim and started asking about his phone, which he had in his hands. Subject 1 then attempted to snatch the phone from the victim. After failing to obtain the phone, subject 1 slashed the victim on his neck with a black utility knife.  Subject 2 took the victim’s backpack from his back and both subjects then fled south through the Quad. The subjects were last seen heading through the 50 Gould Street Archway.

The victim notified Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services of the incident via the Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services Quad Blue Phone. The victim was provided medical assistance by Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services.  Toronto Police and Toronto Ambulance were notified and attended shortly thereafter.

A search of the area was conducted but the subjects were not found.  Ryerson Security and Emergency Services are working with Toronto Police’s on-going investigation into this incident.  The victim’s injuries were not life-threatening and he has been released from the hospital.

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
