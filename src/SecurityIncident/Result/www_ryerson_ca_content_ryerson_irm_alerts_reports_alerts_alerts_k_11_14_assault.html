2014-04-07

Assault

Incident Details:

On Friday 04 April, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 12:35 PM a female non-community member was standing at the northwest corner of Victoria and Dundas Street. The subject approached her from behind and punched her shoulder. The victim pushed the subject away, and managed to defend herself while the subject kept swinging at her. The victim also added she saw the subject earlier around the sidewalk yelling and attempting to provoke passersby into a fight.

Security & Emergency Services attended the area and found the subject in Toronto Police’s custody. The victim was not injured and did not require medical attention.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
