2012-02-16

Pellet Gun Vandalism to Kerr Hall Windows

Incident Details:

Security and Emergency Services would like to alert members of the Ryerson community to a series of incidents where a pellet gun appears to have been used to cause vandalism primarily to windows, as well as a light fixture, in Kerr Hall on several occasions over the past two weeks. 

1.      On Monday 06 February, 2012 at approx. 1:30 pm five pellet-sized holes were found in a door window in a stairwell on the 3rd floor of South Kerr Hall.  Toronto Police were called and attended the area.

2.      On Wednesday 15 February, 2012 at approx. 9:15 am approximately 20 pellet-sized holes were found in three windows in stairwells near the 40 and 50 Gould entrances.  Toronto Police were called again and attended the area.

3.      On Thursday 16 February, 2012 at approx. 11:10 am approximately 17 pellet-sized holes were found in the window and light fixture of a men’s washroom on the lower ground of Kerr Hall South.  Toronto Police were called again and attended the area.

No injuries or other damage has been reported.   Ryerson Security and Emergency Services and Toronto Police are continuing to investigate the incidents as a criminal matter.  

If you have any information about these incidents, or observe suspicious activity, please contact Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or dial “80” from internal phones to report crimes in progress and other emergencies.

Notification Information:

If you are a witness to a crime, please call Ryerson Security at 416-979-5040, or Toronto Police Services through Crime Stoppers at 416-222-TIPS (8477). 

For Emergencies dial "80" (internal phones only).
