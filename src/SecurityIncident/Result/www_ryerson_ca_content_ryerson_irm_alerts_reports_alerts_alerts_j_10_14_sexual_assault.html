2014-03-31

Sexual Assault

Incident Details:

On Friday 28 March, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

At approximately 10:15 PM a community member attended a men’s washroom on the 3rd floor of Kerr Hall building. The victim noticed one of the stalls occupied, and made his way to the urinals. While the victim was using the urinal, the subject exited the stall, approached the victim and grabbed him around his waist. The victim yelled for the subject to stop. The subject removed his hands and retreated back to the stall. The victim soon after left the washroom and notified Security & Emergency Services of the incident.  

Security & Emergency Services checked all the washrooms in the building, but could not locate the subject. The victim, who was not injured, declined Toronto Police involvement at this time.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

Support for students impacted by sexual violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
