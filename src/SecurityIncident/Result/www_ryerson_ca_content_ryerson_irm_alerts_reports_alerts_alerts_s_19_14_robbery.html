2014-07-17

Robbery

Incident Details:

On Wednesday 16 July, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

At approximately 02:30 PM a non-community member was walking east at 297 Victoria Street near the Devonian pond. The subjects approached the victim from behind, and subject 1 snatched his iPod which he had in his hand. The victim turned around and confronted the subject. Subject 1 then punched the victim on the right side of his face and left the area. Both subject 1 and 2 were last seen heading south through the alleyway at the rear of the VIC building. The victim sustained bruising around his right eye and also a laceration on his nose, and was provided medical assistance by Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services.

 

Toronto Police Services attended and took over the investigation. Toronto Emergency Medical Services also attended and escorted the victim to hospital. 

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
