2014-10-08

Voyeurism Suspect Barred from Campus

Incident Details:

On Sunday 05 October, 2014, at approximately 11:35 PM, Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

Minutes earlier, the victim, a Ryerson community member, was getting changed in the south-east women’s washroom, located on the 9th floor of the Ted Rogers School of Management (TRS) building, when she was advised by two witnesses that the subject was peaking inside and watching her change.  Upon overhearing this, the subject entered the washroom and was confronted by the victim, to which he responded by telling the victim that he likes to watch girls.  The victim told the subject to leave the women’s washroom immediately and the subject complied. 

 

The victim reported no physical injuries and declined medical assistance.  Following the response by Security & Emergency Services, a suspect was located and has been barred from campus.  The suspect is not believed to be a Ryerson community member.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
