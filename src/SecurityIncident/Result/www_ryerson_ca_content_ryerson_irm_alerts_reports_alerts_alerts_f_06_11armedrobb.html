2011-03-08

ARMED ROBBERY

Incident Details:

On Saturday 05 March, 2011 a male student reported the following to Security
and Emergency Services:

At approximately 11:05 pm, the victim was walking between Pitman Hall and
the Rogers Communication Centre when two males approached him and asked if
he wanted to buy marijuana.  The victim said he was not interested and kept
walking.  One of the males then produced a knife and held it to the victim's
throat, demanding the contents of the victim's bag.   The males took cash
and alcohol from the bag and then fled the area.   The victim reported the
incident to Security and Emergency Services and Toronto Police were
contacted.

At approx. 11:25 pm Security staff noticed two males who fit the description
of the subjects and they were monitored while Toronto Police were contacted.
Toronto Police took one of the males into custody near Church and McGill
Street.   He was found to be in possession of a prohibited knife.

The victim was not injured during the robbery.  

Suspect information:

The subjects are described as:

Subject #1, Male, dark complexion, 16 years of age, 5'7", 130 lbs, wearing a
dark coloured hooded sweater, a dark blue backpack, dark coloured pair of
pants and a black bandana over his face. 

Subject #2 Male, light complexion, 14-18 years of age, 5'7", 130 lbs, dark
coloured hooded sweater, dark coloured pants, and a black and white bandana
over his face.
