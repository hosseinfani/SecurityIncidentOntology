2014-05-08

Voyeurism

Incident Details:

On Tuesday 06 May, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

At approx. 04:30 PM while a male student was using a washroom stall on the 6th floor of the Library and Archives building, he overhead the subject breathing heavily. Soon after, the student looked up and saw the subject peeking at him through a crack on the stall’s door, for about a minute, while still heavily breathing. The student yelled at the subject who then left the washroom in an unknown direction.

Security & Emergency Services attended the area and conducted a thorough search, but the subject could not be located.

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

Support for students impacted by violence is available through the Centre for Student Development and Counselling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
