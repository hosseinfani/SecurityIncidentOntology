2013-01-08

Assault

Incident Details:

On Monday 07 January, 2013 a male student reported the following incident to Ryerson University Security and Emergency Services at 3 pm:

 

At approximately 10:40 am the student entered the men’s washrooms on the first floor of the Rogers Communication Centre.  The subject was in the washroom standing at the sinks.  When the student entered he said: “What are you looking at?” and then immediately kicked the students in the shin and punched him in the chest.  The student was able to leave the washroom at that time.

 

The student was not physically injured and did not want to file a report with Toronto Police Services at this time.  Security and Emergency Services are investigating the incident.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

 

Suspect information:
