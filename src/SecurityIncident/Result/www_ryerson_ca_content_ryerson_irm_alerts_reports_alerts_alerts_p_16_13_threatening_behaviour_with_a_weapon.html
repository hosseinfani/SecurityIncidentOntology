2013-08-19

Threatening Behaviour with a Weapon

Incident Details:

On Thursday 15 August, 2013 Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 4:33 pm a staff member observed the subject brandishing a black pocket knife and approaching community members sitting on 380 Victoria Street planters. Furthermore, the subject was reported as having approached an individual sitting on the planters and threatened to cut him

 

Toronto Police Services were contacted and attended the area. The male subject was last seen heading towards the Dundas Street subway station. No victims came forward to report the incident.

 

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
