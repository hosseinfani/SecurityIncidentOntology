2012-10-01

Threatening Behaviour

Incident Details:

On Friday 28 September, 2012 at approximately 7:40 pm, the following incident was reported to Ryerson University Security and Emergency Services:

 

Two female Ryerson students reported that at approximately 7 pm, they observed a man enter the first floor of Kerr Hall West through the 379 Victoria entrance who was carrying a knife in his left hand.  He approached the students and said he had “cut someone”, and would “cut” them too. The man then entered a nearby washroom.  The students left the immediate area, and observed him leaving the building near 31 Gerrard Street approximately 35 – 45 minutes later and called Security.

 

Toronto Police Services were notified, and a search of the area was conducted.  The man was not located.

 

If you have any information about these incidents or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.   

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

 

Suspect information:

Male  

Dark complexion   

Between 50-60 years of age

Heavy build with a large belly

Approximately 6' 3" (1m 90 cm) tall

Short black hair, black beard

Wearing a large black coat, black pants, and a grey toque

Scar on upper lip, missing teeth

Overall unkempt appearance
