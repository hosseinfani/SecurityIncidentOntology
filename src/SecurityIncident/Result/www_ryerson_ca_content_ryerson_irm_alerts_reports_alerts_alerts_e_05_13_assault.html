2013-04-15

Assault

Incident Details:

On Thursday 11 April, 2013 Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 06:30 pm a female student was walking by the Victoria and Gould Street intersection when she observed a man and a woman walking eastbound along Gould Street, on the North sidewalk. The man, who was walking ahead of the woman, abruptly stopped causing the woman to bump into him. The student heard the woman apologize to the male subject. The subject then turned around and began yelling and invading the woman’s personal space. The victim attempted to walk past the subject, who reached out with his right hand and grabbed the victim around her neck area and pushed her back. The victim managed to walk past the subject who followed her while yelling and screaming. The victim distanced herself and was last seen heading eastbound along Gould Street.

 

The female student who reported the incident noted no visible injuries on the victim.

 

If you have any information about this incident, whether or not you are the victim of this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
