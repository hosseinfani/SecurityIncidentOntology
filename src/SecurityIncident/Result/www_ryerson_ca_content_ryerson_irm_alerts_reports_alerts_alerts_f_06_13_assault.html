2013-04-25

Assault

Incident Details:

On Friday 19 April, 2013 Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 10:48 pm a male student was walking south on Victoria Street, by Gould Street. The subject, who was walking north suddenly moved towards him, at the last minute, bumped him on his right shoulder and proceeded northbound towards Gerrard Street. There was no interaction between the subject and the victim, before and after the assault. The student approached Security and notified them of the assault.  The area was searched but the man was not located.

 

The victim complained of soreness to his shoulder but did not require medical attention. Additionally, the victim did not wish to file a report with Toronto Police Services at this time.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
