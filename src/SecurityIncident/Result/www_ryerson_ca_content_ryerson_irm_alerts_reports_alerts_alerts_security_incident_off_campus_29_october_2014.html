2014-10-29

Robbery

Incident Details:

On Tuesday 28 October, 2014, at approximately 3:00 AM Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

Approximately one hour earlier, the victim, a Ryerson community member, was approached by three subjects on the north side of the George Vari Engineering and Computing Centre (ENG) on the south sidewalk of Gould Street.  The subjects asked the victim if he “wanted to hang out with them” and the victim declined.  One subject persisted in trying to convince the victim to join them as he placed his hands on the victim’s shoulder from behind.  The victim pushed the subject away and left the area, walking eastbound towards the International Living and Learning Centre (ILC).  At this time, the victim realized he was missing his wallet, which he had placed in his right coat pocket.  The victim then contacted Toronto Police Services and Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services to report the incident.

 

The victim was unable to provide a detailed description of the subjects, who were last observed walking north-bound on Church Street and then west-bound on the north sidewalk of Gerrard Street.  The victim reported no physical injuries, declined medical assistance, and Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services conducted safety planning with the victim.
