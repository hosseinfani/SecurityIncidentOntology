2014-09-30

Sexual Assault – Suspect Barred from Campus

Incident Details:

 

On Friday 26 September, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident taking place near the Devonian Pond along Gould Street:

 

At approximately 03:20 PM a female student reported the subject approaching random female community members and repeatedly trying to hold their hands while attempting to engage them in conversation.

 

Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services promptly attended and located the subject in the area with another female student. Upon investigation, the student informed security the subject had approached her, and held her hand and would not let go. The subject then began talking about sex and about his genitals making the student feel uncomfortable. The student did not want to press criminal charges.

 

Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services approached the subject, who was a non – community member and barred him from Ryerson University. The subject was last seen heading South on Victoria Street. Toronto Police Services was informed of the incident.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
