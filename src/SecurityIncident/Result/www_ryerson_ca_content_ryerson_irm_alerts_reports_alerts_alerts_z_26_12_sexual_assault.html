2012-08-30

Sexual Assault

Incident Details:

On Thursday 30, 2012 the following incident was reported to Ryerson University Security and Emergency Services:

 

Shortly after 2 am, a male sexually assaulted a female student on the patio of the Ram in the Rye pub.  The male was making unwanted advances towards her, and when she indicated she was not interested he grabbed her breast.  The female student was able to escape, entered the pub and summoned assistance. 
 
The male ended up engaged in a physical altercation on the pub patio shortly thereafter.  Toronto Police attended and took a male suspect into police custody.  The victim was not injured. 
 
The man arrested was identified as a Ryerson student.  The incident is under further investigation.

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security. 
 
Support for students impacted by sexual assault is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
