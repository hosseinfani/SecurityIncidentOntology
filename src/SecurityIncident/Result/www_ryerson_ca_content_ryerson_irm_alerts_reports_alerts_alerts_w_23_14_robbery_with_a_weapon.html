2014-07-29

Robbery with a Weapon

Incident Details:

On Saturday 26 July, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

At approximately 05:30 PM two non-community members met with the subjects at the rear of 100 Bond Street to purchase 2 hooded sweaters. The subjects then informed the victims they had the sweaters at a different location and had to leave the area to get them. Subject 1 then demanded the victims give him the cash, which one of the victims’ had in his hand. Subject 2 at the same time informed the victims he would hit them with a rum bottle he had with him, should they refuse to give subject 1 the cash. The victims complied with the demand, and soon after the subjects fled the area and were last seen heading East through the 113 Bond Street alleyway towards Church Street.

The victims were not physically injured during the incident. Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services searched the area for the subjects, but could not locate them. Toronto Police Services attended and took over the investigation.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
