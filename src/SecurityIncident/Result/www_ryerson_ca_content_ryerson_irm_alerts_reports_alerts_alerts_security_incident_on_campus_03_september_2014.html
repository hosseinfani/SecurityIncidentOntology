2014-09-03

Suspect Arrested - Indecent Behaviour

Incident Details:

On Friday 29 August, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 02:45 PM a community member was studying in the Library and Archives building on the 7th floor at a cubicle. The victim then noticed the subject sitting on the floor, underneath the adjacent cubicle. The victim immediately yelled at the subject and notified Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services.

 

Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services attended the area and, located the suspect and arrested him for trespassing as he had been barred from campus previously for an unrelated occurrence, and turned him to Toronto Police Services who took over the investigation. The suspect is believed to be the same person, who had engaged in similar behaviour at the Library and Archives building on Wednesday 20 August, 2014 (http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/alerts_reports/alerts/alerts/Security_Incident_on_Campus_21_August_2014_3.html) 

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
