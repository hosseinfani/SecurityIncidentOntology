2014-04-14

Attempted Armed Robbery

Incident Details:

On Friday 11 April, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 10:30 PM a student was seated on the couches across the Hub in the Podium building facing Victoria Laneway. The subject approached the victim from behind and placed her arms around his neck while holding a sharp object to his neck. The subject demanded the victim give her his laptop and reached in for it. The victim pulled the laptop away from her and the subject fled the area and was last seen heading towards Gerrard Street.  The victim sustained an abrasion to the neck during the brief struggle.

 

Toronto Police Services attended the area and initiated an investigation. The victim was provided first aid at the scene by Ryerson's Security & Emergency Services staff and declined further medical assistance.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
