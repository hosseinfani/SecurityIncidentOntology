2014-08-18

Assault

Incident Details:

On Saturday 16 August, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 11:00 PM a non-community member was sitting on the Ram in the Rye Pub patio, which was closed, reading a newspaper. The victim was then approached by 4 subjects (2 males and 2 females), who at the time were consuming alcohol. The subjects asked the victim for a lighter and the victim said no. The subjects then pushed the victim to the ground and began punching and kicking him. The victim called for help and the subjects at the time fled in an unknown direction.

 

The victim suffered minor injuries and was taken to hospital by Toronto Emergency Medical Services. Toronto Police Services also attended and took over the investigation. Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services searched the area for the subjects but could not locate them. Only vague descriptions were provided for the subjects.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

Notification Information:

If you are a witness to a crime, please call Ryerson Security at 416-979-5040, or Toronto Police Services through Crime Stoppers at 416-222-TIPS (8477). 

For Emergencies dial "80" (internal phones only).
