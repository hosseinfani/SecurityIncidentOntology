2014-02-04

Voyeurism

Incident Details:

On Monday 03 February, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident at 06:00 PM:

 

At approximately 01:00 PM a male student was using the men’s washroom on the third floor of the podium building near the library. The subject, who was inside a stall, reached over top of the stall and took a photo of the student.  The student confronted the man, who then deleted the photo from his cell phone and left walking southbound.

 

The student was not physically injured.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by sexual violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

Suspect information:

Male

Light Complexion

5’9” / 1m 75 cm tall

Approximately 18-25 years of age

Dark short hair

Scruffy dark facial hair

Wearing jeans, a brown Canada Goose jacket, carrying a backpack

Overall neat appearance, well-spoken
