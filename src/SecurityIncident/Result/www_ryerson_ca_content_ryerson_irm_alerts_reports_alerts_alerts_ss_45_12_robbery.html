2012-12-04

Robbery

Incident Details:

On Monday 03 December, 2012 at 6:35 pm the following incident was reported to Ryerson Security and Emergency Services:

 

At approximately 4:05 pm a female student was walking Westbound on the North sidewalk of Gerrard Street towards Yonge Street.  She found a $5 bill on the sidewalk, and enquired of passerby’s if the money belonged to them.  There was a group of four or five males in the area; one of them claimed the money was his but the others said it was not.  The student kept walking.  One of the males followed her and grabbed the money out of her hand.

 

The victim was not physically harmed. The victim has been asked to file a report with Toronto Police Services.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
