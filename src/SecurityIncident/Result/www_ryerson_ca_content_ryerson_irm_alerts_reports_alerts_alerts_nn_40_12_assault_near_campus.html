Assault near Campus

Incident Details:

On Monday 01 October, 2012 at approximately 9:15 pm the following incident was reported to Ryerson University Security and Emergency Services:

 

A female student was walking near the corner of Gould and Mutual Street when a man approached her and asked for money.  She told him she didnâ€™t have any.  The man persisted, and then slapped her across her face.  He fled the area going northbound on Mutual Street.

 

The victim was not physically injured. A search of the area was conducted but the man was not located.  The victim did not wish to involve police at this time and could provide only a vague description of the subject.

 

If you have any information about these incidents or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counselling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
