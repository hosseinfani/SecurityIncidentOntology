2013-11-07

Assault

Incident Details:

On Wednesday 06 November, 2013 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 08:30 PM a community member reported an assault she had witnessed on the Mutual Street sidewalk by the 99 Gerrard Street entrance. They reported a female victim had been walking north on Mutual Street with the subject following close behind her. The subject then charged at the victim striking her on the right side of her head. The subject and the victim were then observed talking, and soon after the subject left northbound on Mutual Street. The victim approached the reporter and appeared visibly distraught, although no injuries were noted. The victim advised the reporter the subject had threatened to stab her if she went to the Police, and added she did not know the subject. 

 

The reporter offered to contact Toronto Police Services for the victim, although she declined and left the area. The reporter further advised that community members in the area informed her the subject is often seen in the area. Security & Emergency Services attended and searched for both the subject and victim, but could not locate them.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
