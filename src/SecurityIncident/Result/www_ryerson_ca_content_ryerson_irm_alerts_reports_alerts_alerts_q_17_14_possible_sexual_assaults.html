2014-05-12

Possible Sexual Assaults

Incident Details:

Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services have been made aware of an allegation that the Subject described below has sexually assaulted one community member, and may have committed acts of sexual assault against additional members of the Ryerson University community.  The Subject often hands out free newspapers near the Tim Horton’s on Victoria Street.  He is alleged to have assaulted women who pass him by hugging, kissing and touching them without their consent.  Toronto Police Services are aware of these allegations.

 

If you have any information about these incidents or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca to file a report. 

 

For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by sexual violence is available through the Centre for Student Development and Counselling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
