2012-09-17

Sexual Assault

Incident Details:

On Sunday 16 September, 2012 a female student reported the following to Ryerson University Security and Emergency Services:

 

On 15 September, 2012 the student was at the Ram in the Rye Pub, when at around midnight a male, who was not known to her, approached her on the dance floor and began “grinding” into her, and then tried to kiss her.  She told the male she had to go, and left the dance floor.  Approximately 10 minutes later the man approached her again, pushed her into the dance floor area and sexually assaulted her by touching her under her clothing. 

 

Toronto Police Services were called and took a report. No detailed description of the man is available.

 
If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.   

 

Support for students impacted by sexual assault is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

Notification Information:

If you are a witness to a crime, please call Ryerson Security at 416-979-5040, or Toronto Police Services through Crime Stoppers at 416-222-TIPS (8477). 

For Emergencies dial "80" (internal phones only).
To see all Security Incidents, please visit our web site at www.ryerson.ca/security.
