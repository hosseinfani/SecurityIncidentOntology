2013-10-16

Assault Bodily Harm

Incident Details:

On Friday 11 October, 2013 Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 04:30 PM the subject approached a male student at the northeast area of the Quad and asked him “where is my weed?” Soon after, the subject grabbed him and stated “You better have my weed or pay me money”. The subject then assaulted the victim, striking his face several times. The subject then made several verbal threats of violence, including a threatened use of a firearm, though no firearm was apparent. The victim made his way to a nearby washroom, where he activated a security duress pull station notifying Security and Emergency Services.

 

The victim sustained injuries to his face and was provided medical assistance by Security and Emergency Services. Toronto Medical and Police Services were notified of the incident. Security and Emergency Services searched the area for the subject, but were unable to locate him.

  

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
