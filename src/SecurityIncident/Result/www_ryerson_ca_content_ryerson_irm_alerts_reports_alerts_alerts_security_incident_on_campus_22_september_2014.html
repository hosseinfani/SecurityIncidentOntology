2014-09-22

Suspect Arrested - Assault with a Weapon

Incident Details:

On Wednesday 17 September, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services came across the following incident, taking place outside the northwest entrance of the Image Arts building on Gould Street:

 

At approximately 11:10 PM the victim, a male non-community member, and three of his friends were sitting at a table on the Gould Street sidewalk near the above location when the subject, a male non-community member, approached and asked them for a cigarette.  Although everyone at the table declined to give him a cigarette, the subject persisted in continuing to ask for a cigarette, refusing to leave.  An argument ensued between the subject and a witness who was sitting at the table, as the subject became increasingly aggressive and verbally abusive towards her because she would not give him a cigarette.  The subject then struck victim’s head multiple times with a closed fist and threw a patio chair at the victim, as Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services immediately intervened upon arrival, approaching and placing the suspect under arrest. 

 

The victim reported no injuries, declined medical assistance and did not wish to pursue any charges against the suspect.  The suspect was subsequently turned over to Toronto Police Services. Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services barred the suspect from campus.  

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
