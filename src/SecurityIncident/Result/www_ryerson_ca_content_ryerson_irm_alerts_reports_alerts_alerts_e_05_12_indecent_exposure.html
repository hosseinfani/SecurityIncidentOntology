Indecent Exposure

Incident Details:

On Wednesday 08 February, 2012 at approximately 10:15 pm a Ryerson student reported the following incident to Security and Emergency Services:


At approximately 9:50 pm she was walking eastbound on McGill Street, near the Theatre School. She heard someone whistling at her and turned around.

The student then observed the subject standing at the rear of 40 Gerrard Street fully exposed and masturbating. The victim turned around immediately and walked towards the Theatre School.


Security and Emergency Services searched the area but where unable to locate the man.  The victim was unable to get a detailed description due to the time of day and nature of the circumstances.


If you have any information about this incident or are the victim of a similar occurrence, please contact Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040.



Suspect information:

Male

Tanned complexion

About 5'5" 

Thin build

Wearing a black winter hat


Notification Information:

If you are a witness to a crime, please call Ryerson Security at 416-979-5040, or Toronto Police Services through Crime Stoppers at 416-222-TIPS (8477).
