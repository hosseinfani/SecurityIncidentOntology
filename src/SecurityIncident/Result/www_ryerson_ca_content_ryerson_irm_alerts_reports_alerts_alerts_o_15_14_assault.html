2014-04-21

Assault

Incident Details:

On Sunday 20 April, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 12:30 AM two students were walking along Gould Street approaching Yonge Street. The students were confronted by the subjects who were with a group of about 8-10 males. Subject 1 shoulder bumped into one of the students and then began pushing him. The other student intervened and attempted to separate subject 1 and the student. Subject 2 at this time joined, and both subjects began punching the students. The students fled the area soon after and the subjects were last seen at the Yonge and Gould Street area.

 

One of the students suffered apparent minor injuries and declined medical assistance. Toronto Police Services were notified of the assault.  

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
