2014-07-29

Assault

Incident Details:

On Friday 25 July, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

At approximately 03:45 PM a community guest was hosting a class just south of the Devonian Pond. The subject approached the class and began harassing the students, by making derogatory comments. The victim intervened and the subject challenged him to a fight. The victim declined, and the subject grabbed his arm. The victim pushed the subject away while telling him to release his arm. The subject released the arm, and headed west towards Victoria Street while knocking over scooters parked in the area.

Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services attended the area and approached the subject. The subject soon after, punched one of the Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services members and was arrested for assault. Toronto Police Services attended and took over the investigation.

The victims were not physically injured during the incident and no damage was noted on the knocked over scooters.

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
