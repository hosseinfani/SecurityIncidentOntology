2014-01-21

Assault with a Weapon

Incident Details:

On Thursday 16 January, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 05:30 PM a male victim was standing by the parking meter on the south side of Gerrard Street just west of the 99 Gerrard Street entrance. The victim was then bumped by the subject. The victim turned around and confronted the subject. The subject proceeded to the nearby planters and picked up a stick, which he then used to strike the victim. An altercation ensued, and the subject fled south on Mutual Street.

 

The victim did not report any injuries and did not wish to pursue the matter further. Security & Emergency Services patrolled the area, but did not locate the subject. Toronto Police Services were notified of the incident.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
