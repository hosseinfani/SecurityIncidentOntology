2011-02-18

ASSAULT

Incident Details:

On Thursday 17 February, 2011 a female student reported the following to
Security and Emergency Services:

On Wednesday 16 February, 2011 at approx. 11 p.m. the student was walking on
the 2nd floor of Kerr Hall South when a man exited the men's washroom and
said "What's up sexy" to her.   The student ignored him and continued
walking.   The man then followed her down the hallway and grabbed her arm
while walking behind her.   The student was able to escape his grip and ran
away. 

The victim was not injured, and did not wish to report the incident to
police at this time.  Security and Emergency Services are investigating the
incident.   She did not get a good look at the man and only a vague
description was obtained.

If you have information regarding this incident or are the victim of a
similar occurrence, please contact Security and Emergency Services at
416-979-5040.

Notification Information:

If you are a witness to a crime, please call Ryerson Security at 416-979-5040, or Toronto Police Services through Crime Stoppers at 416-222-TIPS (8477). 

For Emergencies dial "80" (internal phones only).
