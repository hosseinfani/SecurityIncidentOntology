2013-06-12

Robbery

Incident Details:

On Tuesday 11 June, 2013 Ryerson University Security and Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 09:20 pm a male student was walking by the Mutual and Gould Street area. The student was approached by the subject at the northwest corner of the intersection. The subject asked the student for a cigarette, but was denied. The subject then told the student to give him the cigarettes; otherwise, he was going to take them. The student attempted to walk away from the subject, but was pushed against the east wall of the 80 Gould Street building. The student gave the subject a cigarette. The subject was last seen heading west on Gould Street. 

 

The student did not wish to file a report with Toronto Police Services.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
