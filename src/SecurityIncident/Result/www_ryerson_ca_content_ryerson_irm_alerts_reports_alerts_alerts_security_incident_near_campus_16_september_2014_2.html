2014-09-16

Robbery

Incident Details:

On Saturday 13 September, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident taking place near 285 Victoria Street at the rear of the building in Victoria Street lane:

 

At approximately 10:30 PM Ryerson Security & Emergency Services located a group of approximately 15 individuals engaged in a physical altercation.  During the fighting a man appeared to have been knocked to the ground and rendered unresponsive.  Several male subjects within the remaining group of individuals who were engaged in the fight were observed rummaging through the victim’s pockets, removing his identification and cell phone before running away, heading northbound towards the Devonian Pond. 

 

Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services attended the area, called an ambulance and Toronto Police Services, and provided medical care.  Toronto Emergency Medical Services attended and took over patient care, and Toronto Police Services attended the area and commenced their investigation.  The victim was taken to hospital with non-life threatening injuries.

 

Ryerson Security & Emergency Services are continuing to assist Toronto Police Services with their investigation.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
