Toronto Police Services Update

Incident Details:

Ryerson Security & Emergency Services would like to inform the Ryerson University community of a man wanted in connection to a series of incidents, some of which occurred near campus. One incident which occurred on 07 August, 2014 was posted on the Ryerson Security & Emergency Services website on 11 August, 2014 (Toronto Police Major News Report – Robbery)

 

Please click the link below to view the Toronto Police News release:

http://www.torontopolice.on.ca/newsreleases/release.php?id=29842

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For Emergencies or incidents in progress dial "80" (internal phones only).
