2014-01-27

Assault

Incident Details:

On Wednesday 22 January, 2014 Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

At approximately 11:02 AM a male student was standing just outside the 99 Gerrard Street main entrance doors, while the subject was standing on the planters just west of the entrance. The student looked at the subject, who then started yelling at him. Shortly after, the subject began spitting on vehicles parked along Gerrard Street and approached the student. The subject spat on the student’s jacket and punched him on his shoulder. Security & Emergency Services patrolling the area overhead the yelling and attended the area. The subject on noticing Security & Emergency Services fled, and was last seen heading southbound on Jarvis Street.

 

The victim was not injured and did not wish to pursue the matter further. Security & Emergency Services patrolled the area, but did not locate the subject.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
