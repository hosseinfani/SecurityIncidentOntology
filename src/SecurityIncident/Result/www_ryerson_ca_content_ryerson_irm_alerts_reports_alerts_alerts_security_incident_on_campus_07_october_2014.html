2014-10-07

Assault

Incident Details:

 

On Saturday 04 October, 2014, at approximately 11:35 AM, Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident:

 

Approximately twenty-five minutes earlier, the victim, a Ryerson community member, was approached and asked for spare change by the subject next to the SHE building on the south sidewalk of Gerrard Street.  The victim told the subject that he had no change to give and began walking away when the subject followed and struck the victim on the side of his face, before beginning to walk eastbound on Gerrard Street towards Jarvis Street.

 

The victim reported no physical injuries, declined medical assistance and did not want Toronto Police Services involved.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services attended the area and last observed a suspect heading eastbound on Gerrard Street towards George Street.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services are investigating this incident.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
