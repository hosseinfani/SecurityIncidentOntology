2013-09-03

Aggravated Assault

Incident Details:

Toronto Police Services issued a “Major News Report” on 03 September, 2013 about an incident that we would like to inform the Ryerson community about.  The following was posted on the Toronto Police website:

 

“An 18 year old male reports that on August 31, 2013 at approximately 0040 hours, he was in the area of Gould Street and Victoria Street when he was approached by four male suspects.  One suspect stabbed the victim and the victim managed to make good his escape.  The suspects then fled the scene in an unknown direction.  The victim was transported to a hospital by Toronto EMS for treatment.  Police are requesting the assistance of the public in identifying the following described persons in connection with this offence.  Description of Suspects #1 to #2: Male, black, 22 to 26 years, thin build, short black hair.  Suspects #3 to #4: Male, black, 22 to 26 years, thin build.”

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security. 

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counseling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).
