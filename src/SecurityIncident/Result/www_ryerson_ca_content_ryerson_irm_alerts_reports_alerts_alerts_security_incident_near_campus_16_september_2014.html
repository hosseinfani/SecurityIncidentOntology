2014-09-16

Suspicious Behaviour

Incident Details:

On Friday 12 September, 2014 Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services were made aware of the following incident taking place near the International Living Learning Centre, just north of the intersection of Gould Street and Mutual Street.

 

At approximately 02:30 PM a male community member was walking from the Rogers Communication Centre to the International Living Learning Centre, when a vehicle travelling northbound, described as a dark green Jeep bearing a Quebec licence plate, stopped on Mutual Street and Gould Street, and the driver asked the victim for directions.  After the victim provided the subject with directions, the subject engaged the victim in conversation, claiming that he was a fashion designer, recently had a show in Toronto, and then offered the victim some free clothing. 

 

The victim agreed and the subject parked and exited his vehicle on the east side of Mutual Street.  The subject opened the rear door on the driver’s side, showed the victim two black bags in the back of his car and told the victim to get into the vehicle to look at the clothes.  Although the victim had told the subject that he did not want to get into the vehicle, the subject remained persistent about the victim getting into the car.  At this time the victim told the subject that he did not need any free clothes and then left, going into the International Living Learning Centre before contacting Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services.  Toronto Police Services have been informed.

 

If you have any information about this incident or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca. For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Ryerson University Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Walk Safe escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at http://www.ryerson.ca/irm/security/index.html.
