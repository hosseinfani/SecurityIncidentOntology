2012-10-02

Robbery

Incident Details:

On Monday 01 October, 2012 at approximately 11:10 am the following incident was reported to Ryerson University Security and Emergency Services:

 

A female student was walking down the stairs towards the lower ground of the Jorgenson Hall near the 350 Victoria entrance.  Two men were walking upwards, and while passing her grabbed and stole her purse. They continued walking up and left in an unknown direction.

 

The victim was not physically injured. A search of the area was conducted but the men were not located.  The victim did not wish to involve police at this time and could provide only a vague description of the subjects.

 

If you have any information about these incidents or have been the victim of a similar incident, please call Security and Emergency Services at 416-979-5040 or via email at security@ryerson.ca.  For any incidents in progress use the Emergency "80" number from internal phones.  Security & Emergency Services offers 24 hour Safe Walk escorts and free self-defense courses; please check our website for more information at www.ryerson.ca/security.  

 

Support for students impacted by violence is available through Student Development and Counselling (416-979-5195, http://www.ryerson.ca/counselling/).

 

Notification Information:

If you are a witness to a crime, please call Ryerson Security at 416-979-5040, or Toronto Police Services through Crime Stoppers at 416-222-TIPS (8477). 

For Emergencies dial "80" (internal phones only).
